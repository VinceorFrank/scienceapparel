üîÑ Navigation Flow & User Experience

Entry Points

Users can enter the site through the Home page (/), Products page (/products), or direct product links (/product/:id). The header provides consistent navigation with hamburger menu, logo, search, cart, and language toggle.

Product Discovery

Users can browse products through the main Products page, category-specific pages (Clothing & Accessories, Accessories), or use the search functionality. Product cards show images, prices, and add-to-cart buttons.

Shopping Experience

Users can add products to cart from any product page, view their cart (/cart), adjust quantities, and proceed to checkout. Guest users can shop without registration, while logged-in users get additional features.

Authentication Flow

Users can register (/signup) or login (/login) to access customer features. After authentication, they can access their account (/account), order history (/orders), and profile management (/account/edit).

Order Management

Logged-in customers can view order details (/order/:id), track orders (/order-tracking), and complete payments (/payment/:orderId). The system provides order confirmation and tracking information.

Admin Access

Administrators access the admin panel through /admin/login. The admin dashboard (/admin/dashboard) provides access to all management functions including products, orders, users, and analytics.
